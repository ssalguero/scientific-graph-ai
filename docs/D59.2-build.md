## 1. Pipeline implementado

```text
Pointer
  ↓
Drag Math (origin + delta → candidate)
  ↓
SnapTargetProviders.collect (Workspace + Window)
  ↓
WindowSnapEngine.applyMagneticSnap
  ↓
WindowGeometryState.set
```

```text
Engine ──► ∅  (sigue sin conocer Drag / Resize / Manager)
DragBridge ──► compose ──► Engine
```
